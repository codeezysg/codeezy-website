About Codeezy

Start Easy, Build Big!

Founded in 2025 by two friends who are passionate to make a change, Codeezy's vision is to make coding accessible and enjoyable for every child, providing an easy entry into the world of technology.

We believe in empowering young learners to start simple and build big, transforming their curiosity into real-world skills that will shape their future. By fostering creativity, confidence, and problem-solving abilities, we aim to cultivate the next generation of tech innovators, leaders, and changemakers

Why Choose Us?

Why You Should Start Learning with Us?

We strive to empower every student with the confidence and skills to turn their ideas into reality, preparing them for a future where technology is key to success.

Approachability

We understand that for many parents and students, coding can seem intimidating. That's why we break down complex concepts into easy-to-understand lessons that feel more like fun challenges than technical hurdles. At Codeezy, we believe learning to code should never be overwhelming, but rather a series of small, enjoyable wins.

Hands-On Learning

We believe the best way to learn is by doing. Our courses are hands-on and interactive, allowing students to see their progress firsthand. Whether they’re building games, creating apps, or solving real-world problems, we provide students with the tools and opportunities to apply what they’ve learned in meaningful ways.

Real-World Relevance

Our curriculum is designed with the future in mind. The skills your child learns today are the same skills that will be in demand tomorrow. By giving students the tools to understand and create with technology, we are setting them up for success in a world that is increasingly driven by innovation and digital literacy.
